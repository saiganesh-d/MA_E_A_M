Programme Overview

The online M.A. in Electoral Analysis and Management explores how elections, political campaigns, and democratic institutions shape modern governance and political decision-making. It examines the dynamics of electoral systems and their influence on leadership, public policy, and democratic participation.

Offered by the Jindal School of Government and Public Policy (JSGP) and the Jindal School of International Affairs (JSIA), the programme brings together perspectives from political science, public policy, and data analytics to understand electoral processes and political strategy.

Students explore how electoral systems influence governance, how campaigns and political communication shape voter engagement, and how data, research, and public opinion analysis are used to understand election outcomes and political behaviour.
